Exit tickets

At the end of each class meeting, we'll ask you to take 1-2 minutes to fill out this Exit Ticket (you can also click the giant purple button on the right sidebar). Filling this out helps your instructional team and GA create a more effective learning experience for you.

Homework

We'll be assigning homework every week, due each Thursday of class. You'll get feedback the week after you hand it in.
